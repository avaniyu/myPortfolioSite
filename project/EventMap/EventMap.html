EventMap

An interactive physical installation for tourists in public space to visualize upcoming events in Stockholm. 

We exhibited EventMap in ACM TEI 2018 conference as a part of Student Design Challenge, and got many positive feedbacks.

Visit our conference abstract and GitHub repo

When travelling to a new city...

Tourists want to know recent city events on diverse topics to experience local life,
they prefer customizing their own travel experience,
and wish to have a city bird-view.
In addition, tourists come from different backgrounds, deserve walk-up-and-use experience.

Oki, let's do a proto

A table with a map of Stockholm city on its surface. 

Work presented by me, Nicolaas Peter Bijman, Andre Josefsson and Jim Tolman in 8 weeks | Sept. - Oct. 2017
I was main responsible of electronics, and participated in interaction design, software development and project presentation
Project completed in the course “Physical Interaction Design” under the instruction of Mattias Jacobsson and Jordi Solsona Belenguer
