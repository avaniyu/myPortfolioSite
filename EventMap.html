EventMap

An interactive installation that feeds tourists incoming to Stockholm city with local event information by providing them with physical interaction and printed-out take-away.

How to open a city in a local manner?

Have you ever experienced a moment that travelling to a new city, you would like to know how's the overall city look like? What interesting events are happening around? You can navigate over plain city map on mobile phone, check tips from TripAdvisor, but still feel the distance with this city - you are not alone that feel so.
